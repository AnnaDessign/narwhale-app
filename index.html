Awesome Features

Control your digital assets while on the go with Narwhal wallet for iOS and Android.
In addition, using the wallet you get the following

Highly secure

All transactions are encrypted and securely stored.

Multi-signature

Allows you to create and confirm multi-signature transactions.

Don't need Internet

You can use the wallet even without connecting to the Internet.

Nice design

The funny Narwhal character will cheer you up every time you use the app.

Easy Import & Export of Wallets

Adding a new or existing wallet is very easy. Just a couple of clicks and you're done.
Just as easy to delete and rename wallets.

Two Modes of Operation - Online and Offline

Only a small number of cryptocurrency wallets support this feature. And Narwhal is one of them. Now you don't need two different wallets for different modes of operation.
